Let's Play Pig Dice!


Enter user names and a score to play up to or accept the default names & score. Each player will take turns "Rolling the Dice." Each roll will add to the current turns's total. If a 1 is rolled, the current roll total will be 0 and the turn will switch to the other player. If a player wants to save their current turn's total they can "Hold Score" and the turn will switch to the other player. Players will continue with this until a player's total score is equal to or greater to the point goal selected.
